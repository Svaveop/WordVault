# Word Vault 📘

A simple, clean web app to store words and translations. Search, hide/show translations, and export your list to JSON. Completely free, ad-free, and distraction-free - just your vocabulary.

---

## Features

- Add, edit, and delete words with translations  
- Instant search and filtering  
- Hide or show all translations with one click  
- Export your vocabulary to JSON for backup or sharing  
- Persistent storage in your browser (your words stay saved)  
- Smooth, modern, and scrollable UI  

---

## Usage

1. Open `index.html` in your browser  
2. Enter a word and its translation, then click **Add**  
3. Use the search bar to quickly find words  
4. Click the **language icon** to hide/show all translations  
5. Click **Export JSON** to save your list  

---

## About

Word Vault is unique: no ads, no subscriptions, no distractions - just your words, perfectly organized.
